Rainbow

Rainbow accepts up to 5 comma separated tracks and generates a playlist by filling in transitions from track to track. Tracks entered are searched and then are manually verified in the next page. There is also an option to export created playlists to spotify. I would recommend trying only 2 tracks for your first run, as 3 or more may take several minutes to run

An example run of this might look like: (and you can copy paste this into the box above to test the form!)

Island in The Sun, Ms. Jackson, Day 'N' Nite

This would be a great way to create a playlist containing Island in the Sun by Weezer, Ms. Jackson by Outkast, and Day 'N' Nite by Kid Cudi all in one playlist with tracks that can bridge the gaps between these songs.
